The Artificial Era - The Dark Time of Machines leaves the reader apprehensive with some situations that occur in a catastrophic scenario engulfed by destruction. And this leads one to ponder: will we ever witness the dreaded technological singularity?

Author Information

Diego Souza is passionate about technology and works in Web Development. He is also an eternal fan of science fiction and a writer in his spare time. A true "Carioca," he loves Rio de Janeiro, the city where he was born.

The book The Artificial Era presents an apocalyptic scenario in which robots have gained independence and thus become the dominant species on the planet. How can humanity confront this powerful enemy?

These and more details can be seen in the science fiction story that promises to make you reflect on the future of humanity!
